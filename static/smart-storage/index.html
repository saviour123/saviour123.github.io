5. MyCloud

This is offered by Western Digital (WD). This company is one of the biggest if not biggest of external drive manufacturers. With one app you can connect other apps whether installed or not installed on your phone.The new Auto Backup feature helps preserve the memories on your mobile device. You have instant access to all changes you make. You can optionally upgrade your account with a monthly or yearly subscription to a pCloud Premium 500GB and pCloud Premium Plus 1TB plan.

Backup never-ending contacts, messages, photos, videos, music, documents, call logs,phone settings, wow this is a great app. Just select what you wish to protect and you're done!
