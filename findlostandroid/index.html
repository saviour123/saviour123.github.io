Locate Android Phone

Hi all,

I get a lot of posts about lost or stolen phones, so I wanted to share a few tips with you on locating your missing android device.

I have personally used these basic steps to track my moms missing cell phone, that's the good news!

1. AndroidLost or plan b.

This can be remotely installed from a PC with your android gmail account from the Playstore.

Depending on your android version, install the plan b or androidlost.

With android lost, navigate to androidlost.com

And see several commands including activating your GPS by just a text message.

This works even if your phone is stolen. You can remotely install it and track your missing phone as well as get text messages from your missing phone. Its a light weight app all android users can and must install.

2. Avast anti-theft.

The main caution here is to look out for avast anti-theft and not antivirus!

This also gives you a real time tracking and is best to install it before the phone gets missing. It can prevent the phone from accepting a factory reset. Isn't this wonderful!

so it's simple, when you misplace or lose your cell phone through theft, just go to avast.com, sign in with your account and see the location of your phone on the map.

3. Android Device Manager.

I least recommend this, but you can still install it on your phone as well. Just like the way you cannot downplay the essense of internet explorer on your laptop even though it is actually redundant.

It allows you to see the location of your phone. It is also helpful in helping to track your partner. All that you need is the gmail account of the particular phone in question together with it's password. The software allows you to track your missing phone from another android device.

Pls. Note.

Go to location setting and enable it.

Install at least 2 of the 3 apps. They are all light weight apps usually not more than 4mb from the playstore.

Also open and register your avast once you install it as well as the androidlost.

If your phone is missing, there is hope with androidlost Avast anti-theft and the android device manager. It is expedient to try tracking your phone very early before the thief gets smart. Again, i advice you dont wait for your phone get stolen before you think of securing it; of course where will the phone be by then!

Save a friend from going through the trauma of a missing or stolen phone by Sharing with them.
