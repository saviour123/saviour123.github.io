Recover Files from a Corrupted Pendrive

Your Files on Pendrive

Pendrives are no longer safe because of ubiquitous Skypee viruses, shortcut viruses and auto run issues that seem to corrupt Pendrives. It's annoying when all of a sudden your files disappear on the Pendrive. The solution is finally here. I would not only explain how the virus work but also help you to restore your files.

Let's go:

  1. DO NOT format your Pendrive because of virus. You heard me right. Formatting would erase everything on the drive including your personal files (hidden files) which will put you on the losing end. It renders this approach totally impossible. If you have formatted your drive, sorry this post is not for you.

  2. Proceed and click on Start or (Windows Key and Q) Search “show hidden files without” without the quotes.

  1. When the 'View hiden files' opens, click on show hidden files. Uncheck Hide Operating System Files (recommended) accept the warning by YES- viruses are usually configured to be hidden as one of the operating system files-. Click Ok.

  1. Proceed to open your Pendrive in the windows explorer, now your files are visible but light coloured. You can manually copy the files and create a new directory or folder on the Pendrive and paste them into it. You can then delete the hidden folder.
Alternatively (hacker edition).

While the infected Pendrive is plug in, pull up CMD/Command Prompt or PowerShell. Change the drive letter to the one your pc have assigned your Pendrive. Eg F, G etc. For drive letter eg. G Use the command: G: <>

Now your drive is active.

Type: attrib -s -h /s /d

At this point, all hidden files get pulled up. They show up again.

Run check disk for the storage media/pendrive. chkdsk /f Accept the process: Type y and hit enter.

Thanks to Attribute command, your files are restored to your storage media (memory card, pendrive, mp3 player, external hdd etc.)
