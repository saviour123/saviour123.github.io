Love

The beauty of love

That lies within

Is a precious seed

Planted by Him

That grows to become

A beautiful rose,

Touching lives of others

As it comes and goes.

Inspiration drawn from a daughter

Smiles to kindle our day

Warm hugs to melt our hearts in Love Created by God.

Love beyond Love

Love beyond desperation

Love beyond imagination

The thoughts of not seeing you at Church.

But Fathers Love remains supreme.

It conquers fear and inscribe Confidence in your heart.

The Grace of Lord

Overshadow you and radiates you into perpetual Glory and Good Health.

God Bless You.

Remember Someone cares.
